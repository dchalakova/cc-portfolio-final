Reflection on my Relationship with Tech


My relationship with technology and tech is complicated. I grew up thinking I'd go into computer science, so I've always enjoyed coding conceptually,

a safe space that I enjoy exploring and dream of making a career in, hence why I'm here at the MFADT program at Parsons!
