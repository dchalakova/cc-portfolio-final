Project 4: Exquisite C

PROCESS:


We all respectively drew our different characters, as seen in the assets folder included. I decided to take Ryan's head, Jovana's body, and Shawki's leg to create my character. We didn't really
share background stories of our characters, but Ryan mentioned that his character was inspired by his lunch, with the head being a meatball and the little top being a mushroom.

BACKGROUND STORY:


A background story I've thought of for this character is that he's a mischevious guy who loves to eat mushrooms, only moves on the X-axis, and never shows his hands.

REFLECTION:

make it seem disjointed and clearly from 3 different characters, it made it seem uniquely cartoon-y. This was also a great project for function practice and comprehension, since if I had written
